About PharmaShelf

PharmaShelf is a simple web application designed to help pharmacy teams quickly look up basic drug information, check stock availability, review alternatives, and perform basic interaction checks.

This project was developed by Dalia Alkassaboghly as part of the AXSOS Full Stack training program. It combines the concept of full stack development concepts including database design, Django, HTML/CSS, and basic UI/UX.
